    • Inadequacy.

    Inadequacy is a disease.
    One cured with flashing lights, dim spaces, broken dreams, fancy colors, and messy rooms.
    Imaginations running wild, of places far away, people you’ve never known. Dreaming in incredible worlds, universes that never existed.
    A soft warmth illuminating your arm. The soft flow of hair through your fingers. A gentle breath you can’t hear.
    A heartbeat that never existed.

    Glance at the numbers. It’s time to go. Maybe you’ll find satisfaction in the words.

    I don’t.

    Perhaps I am inadequate. I look down on others. A disguise.
    But in the end
    We are all victims of time.

    And maybe today I’ll never be as good as someone’s yesterday. But that’s okay.
    At least I was myself.
