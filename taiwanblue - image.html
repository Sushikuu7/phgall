    I took this image about two years ago, in the summer, maybe fall, of 2018, at a Taiwan airport's noodle shop.
    It captures a slight sense of loneliness and change, an idea of being lost, unsure of what to do in this world.

    As you stare at the rain, falling outside
    that glass window,
    The planes slowly passing by.




    At peace.
    Yet approaching unavoidable change.




    Or she could have just been tired from the long flight :>
