    I took this image about two years ago, at the end of August, 2018, at a restaurant in Taiwan Taoyuan Airport.
    I feel that it captures a slight sense of loneliness and change, being lost, thinking of our purpose in this world.

    You stare at the rain, falling outside
    that glass window,
    The planes slowly passing by.




    At peace.
    Yet approaching unavoidable change.




    Or she could have just been tired from the long flight :>
