    • Self-Awareness.

    I say “despite” too much.
    Hypocritical as such.
    Rhymes bore the audience
    Overdone
    overdone.
    Yet here I stand, typing away
    as hypocritical as the land
    rhyming as I may.
    Nothing really matters, if I am to be true.
    We all die someday.

    There’s no point in writing this, whatever it may be
    No intricate flow, no special verse
    No deeper meaning, just honest words.
    Because I write not to entertain
    but to satisfy my losses
    to hide away the pain.
